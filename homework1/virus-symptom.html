Computer Virus Signs & Symptoms

What is a virus? Virus Types Virus Symptoms Prevention and Removal



A few computer virus symptoms are:

  • 1. The computer and/or internet is suddenly slower
  • 2. The computer freezes or crashes frequently
  • 3. Unusual error message appear or unwanted advertisements
  • 4. Files are randomly deleted or added
  • 5. Emails are sent from your account to your contacts
  • 6. Suddent hardware problems such as display flickering
  • 7. Computer reboots on its own
