What is a virus? Virus Types Virus Symptoms Prevention and Removal



A few prevention tips are:

  • 1. Keep antivirus up to date
  • 2. Firewall should be installed
  • 3. Do not open email that is not from someone you trust
  • 4. Do not install unauthorized or pirated software on your computer
  • 5. Create a good restore point and backup
  • 6. Before installing free software first check that its reliable by reading revies
  • 7. Best protection is never click on suspicious links, never download music, videos or files from a suspicious website.
  • 8. Use a secure and safe web browser like Chrome or Firefox and keep it updated

For computer Virus Detection and Removal:

  • 1. Do a full system scan with your antivirus software
  • 2. Delete temporary files by running Disk Cleanup
  • 3. Run malwarebytes to get rid of malware on your computer
  • 4. Restore your computer to a time before you had the viruses
